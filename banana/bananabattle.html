Banana Battle

Instructions

Two cheeky monkeys are racing to steal each other's bananas. Each knowing the other is not to be trusted, hid their bananas in the jungle. Can you find the bananas? To do so click the big red button and guess where they are. The squares of jungle are A-J on the top and 1-10 on the side. A1, would guess the top left, while A10 would be the bottom. First one to find all the Bananas wins. They are 5 bananas, one little one, two medium sized, one a bit larger, and one big banana. Remember you need to find all of them to win
