About

I'm a full stack web developer based in the San Francisco Area. After studing biology and english at Bowdoin College, I graduated and entered the work force as a clinical research coordinator.
I learned quickly learned the fundamentals of the role and soon found myself in leadership positions, helping educate new staff and taking on some of the responsibilities of project management as well, such as designing study procedures. Still, I soon found myself discontent with the work and without too much room to grow professionally. While I still ensured everything ran smoothly, I started to experiment with online coding courses.

After Hiking the Appalachian Trail and really spending some time reflecting on my future, I decided to enroll at General Assembly's WDI course,

The 12 week immersive helped greatly expand my working knowledge of programming, as well as exposing me to actual coding. After learning the fundamentals, the course became very project based with an emphasis on collaboration. I loved the work environment and the feeling of creative control and it only cemented my desire to web develop for many years to come. In an evergrowing field, I'm excited to keep growing my knowledge base.
